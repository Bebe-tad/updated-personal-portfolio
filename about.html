About Me

✨ About Me

I’m a passionate and driven Computer Science student at the University of Gondar. My tech journey began at a young age, sparked by curiosity and a love for problem-solving. During my teenage years, I was fortunate to receive a summer tech course scholarship at Debark University, which laid the foundation for my growth in the field.

Now, as a third-year student, I continuously strive to expand my knowledge and sharpen my skills. I have hands-on experience in web development, database management, and programming, and I’m always eager to take on new challenges.

This personal portfolio showcases some of the projects I've developed — from responsive websites to database-driven applications — each one representing a step forward in my journey as a developer.

Beyond the screen, I enjoy expressing creativity through crafting — especially making ribbon flower bouquets during my free time. It’s a simple joy that balances my technical pursuits with a touch of artistry.
